HOW TO PLAY

🐉

Flapping & Gravity

Tap, click, or press Space to fly upward. Let go to fall. Balance is key to passing narrow gaps!

🚧

Columns & Gaps

Fly through gaps in moving and wind-blown pillars. Do not hit the tubes or you will crash!

⭐

Collect Stars

Yellow stars inside gaps give +2 standard coins. Combine them with the Magnet to auto-attracted them.

🔥

Pillar Combos

Pass pillars consecutively to grow your Combo. At combo 3x-15x, you earn multiple points & coins per pillar!

🌀

Dynamic Wind

At Level 6+, watch the wind banners carefully! Wind applies sideways force, dragging you left or right.

☄️

Asteroids

At Level 9+, space asteroids will spawn between barriers and drift toward you. Fly or boost to dodge!
